About me

Content that feels like a text from your most stylish friend.

I'm Abiola — Amsterdam-based UGC creator with a background in styling and skincare. I film everything on my phone, in my own home, in my own voice, because that's what makes people trust it. Brands send me the product; I send back a library of raw, usable content: hooks, testimonials, tutorials, and B-roll ready to run as paid ads.

No studio, no script that sounds like a script — just content that performs because it's honest.
